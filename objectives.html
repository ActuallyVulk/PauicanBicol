Lights, Camera, Action! Connecting Hearts Through Performance

OBJECTIVES

Driven to be the BU College of Arts and Letters' leading theater organization, we aim to cultivate a generation of well-rounded performers by fostering artistic growth, cultural appreciation, and social consciousness.

To serve as the premiere theater organization at the BU College of Arts and Letters
To train a pool of students to become holistic and disciplined artists of the nation while promoting culture and arts.
To immerse its members in holistic development and social awareness through its events and productions.

Our Goals

   Equipping students with comprehensive pre-professional arts training, fostering innovative exploration of social issues, and promoting cultural heritage preservation are the cornerstones of our program.

Provide comprehensive pre-professional training in the areas of acting, directing, dance, music, literary and theater technology.
Provide students and extended communities distinctly innovative approaches to research/ creative work in the arts anchored on the millennium development goals.
Enhance students interests in skills in Arts, extension service and production activities.
Inculcate among the students the discipline, dynamism dedication and commitment and the preservation of the cultural heritage of the Bicolanos for national unity and identity.
