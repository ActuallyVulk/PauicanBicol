Luzon's biggest theater festival is now back!


Pista Nin Teatrong Bikolnon 2024 is right around the corner. With Taragbo Bikol Theater Network spearheading, more delegates from Luzon come together to mount a festival, a celebration of progressive and transformative performances on April 4-6 at the Albay Astrodome.

Sining Lila Bicol Spirit of The Glass


Sining Lila Bicol celebrates its 3rd Anniversary mounting a production entitled, "Spirit of the Glass." Last March 14, 2024, at Pasakalye Black Box, Sining Lila commenced their anniversary through media viewing of the play production. PAUICAN is with Sining Lila Bicol in projecting a thought-provoking and transformative art. Para sa malaya at progresibong sining!

Takipsilim Sinag Queen 2024


The Backelor of Performing Arts-Theatre's month-long celebration of the arts, featuring events like Bukangliwayway, Pananghali, and Takip-Silim, was a resounding success, offering both entertainment and enlightenment.

Cagsawa Cultural Night


During the Cagsawa Festival Cultural Night's Opening Salvo, the exceptional students of the Bachelor of Performing Arts (BPeA) Theater took center stage and delivered a spellbinding performance that left the audience in awe.
